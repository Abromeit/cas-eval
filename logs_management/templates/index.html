How to

Setting up is easy and requires only a few minutes. After that you will be helping us by just doing searches and occasionally giving quick feedback.

You can easily opt out from the experiment at any time. Or you can switch from our logging proxy to a normal search engine back and forth without much effort.

Privacy

We collect your data to do research. Ultimately, we want to build a model of user behavior on a Search Engine Result Page and propose a new evaluation metric.

Your data is stored and accessed securely and your privacy is protected. Only you have access to your log. You can delete the queries that you consider privacy-sensitive at any time.

Note that we will be able to use your data only after you explicitly transfer it to us. At this point your search log will be anonymized and shuffled so that there will be no way to identify you.

Overview

In this task, you will be given a question and a small piece of text. Your main task is to judge if the text contains an answer to the question or not.

Time

You will need about half a minute to complete each task. Less once you read and understand the rules.

Notes

IMPORTANT: Answer is not the same as matching words. For instance, for the question [normal body temperature] an answer is needed, not just some random text where words "normal", "body" and "temperature" occur.
IMPORTANT: Prefer partial answer to full answer if in doubt!
HINT: Do not try to open any web links while answering this question.
